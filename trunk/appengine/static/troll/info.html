Troll physics

Current rules

  • The mouse button (or CTRL+mouse button) sets the direction the gravity that affects the arms.
  • When the hands reach high enough velocity, they will be able to strike the faces.
  • A separate score is kept for matching the appropriate arm to the appropriate gender.

Future plans/ideas
